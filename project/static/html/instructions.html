Welcome to Course Match, a platform allowing UNSW students to easily match courses for exchange.

We empower students with an easily accesibly database of courses from UNSW's partner universities.

Begin by adding your courses from UNSW, add any country preferences you may have, then add any university preferences you may have in those countries.

If you already have a university in mind, feel free to ignore the country field.

Once finished, click generate and wait a few moments for our system to gather results.

From here, all you have to do is find the courses at the partner university you want to take. To assist, we have provided a couple of tools:

AI-assisted calculation of similarity between the two courses
A side-by-side comparison of both course outlines.

We hope you find your match!
